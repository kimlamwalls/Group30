Kim lives in Hobart, Tasmania. Has a background in music tech, photography and videography. Hobbies include: video games, rock climbing, cycling, electronics. The spark that introduced Kim to IT came from his father, a photographer who was an early adopter of digital photography. This spark instilled a constant optimism for the ever evolving possibilities that new tech could have on the world. Kim has enjoyed the consistent presence of evolving technologies over the last few decades.
This presence has covered many different disciplines from music, art installations to video and photo capture and processing, social media, exploration of video games and electronics. After all this he has found himself in the presence of these delightful individuals now known as Technodrome Inc.
